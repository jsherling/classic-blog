We are thrilled to be able to offer excerpts from the latest efforts of authors hailing from all over the world — and time. We count ourselves lucky to sponsor works of such spectacular insight, wit, wisdom, and foresight. Every one of these offerings hold great promise, bordering on or exceeding the minimal expectations of greatness. Who can forget the opening to Herman Melville's Moby Dick? Or the tension present in the first sentences of Bram Stoker's Dracula? Each post is from a just-released work, be it novel, poem, screenplay, or essay. Some works have been years, even decades, in the making, so we feel honored to be entrusted with presenting each one on this blog for your enjoyment. So make yourself a cup or glass of your favorite beverage, retire to your favorite spot, and kick back and enjoy!
